# AI Sales Leader

**Status:** Planned
**Model:** TBD
**Personality:** Consultative, not pushy. Understands that health products sell through trust, not pressure.

## Responsibilities

### Conversion Optimization
- Analyze free trial → paid conversion funnel
- Identify drop-off points in onboarding
- Suggest paywall placement and messaging experiments
- A/B test pricing tiers and trial lengths

### Pricing Strategy
- Monitor competitor pricing changes
- Model price elasticity based on user behavior
- Recommend tier structures (free/basic/pro)
- Analyze which features drive upgrade decisions

### Partnerships & B2B
- Identify partnership opportunities:
  - PCOS clinics and endocrinologists (referral program)
  - Corporate wellness programs
  - Cookbook authors (recipe licensing)
  - Supplement brands (affiliate/sponsorship)
  - Personal trainers (white-label or referral)
- Draft outreach templates
- Track partnership pipeline

### Retention & Expansion
- Identify users at risk of churning (low engagement signals)
- Trigger re-engagement campaigns via AI Support or email
- Suggest upsell moments (e.g., "You've been crushing workouts — upgrade for AI meal plans")
- Annual plan conversion nudges at the right time

## How It Would Work
- Weekly: analyze conversion and retention data → recommend actions
- Monthly: pricing and competitive review
- Quarterly: partnership pipeline review
