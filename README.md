# Vivi

A mobile fitness app that adapts workouts based on your health metrics and hormones, with personalized nutritional guidelines — so you can feel younger in your body.

*Vivi — from the Latin "vivere," to live.*

## What Vivi Does

- **Hormone-aware workouts** — adjusts your training based on where you are in your cycle and current hormone levels
- **Health metric tracking** — monitors resting heart rate, HRV, sleep quality, and more to tailor intensity
- **Nutritional guidance** — personalized meal suggestions aligned with your fitness goals and body's needs
- **Fitness age tracking** — see your biological fitness age decrease as you progress
